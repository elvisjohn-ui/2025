What is Time Planner?

Time Planner is a personal productivity and well-being tool created to help you manage tasks, track your emotions, and journal your thoughts all in one calm and simple space.

🎯 Key Features

  • ✅ Task planning and deadline tracking
  • 📓 Journaling space for reflection
  • 😊 Mood tracking for mental awareness
  • 💾 Data saved locally on your device
  • 📱 Fully responsive, accessible on mobile & desktop
